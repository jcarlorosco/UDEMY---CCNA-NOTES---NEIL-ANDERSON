Troubleshooting Methods

Top Down - OSI
Bottom Up - OSI
Divide and Conquer (Upper and lower)- OSI

	- Compare configurations
	- Trace the path (Start from source to destination)
	- Swap out components (swap with working hardware)

Connectivity Troubleshooting Methods

	- Ping (ICMP)
	- Traceroute
	- Telnet (Test if the port is open to the destination)
